Why Learn Pascal?

Honestly just for the fun of it, pascal is an old languange with a pretty interesting syntax that you dont really see now a days, anther reason is that most older academic computer science books use pascal so if you ever need to pick one up you won't be complely lost. But by far the best reason is that learning programming languanges is a skill, the more practice you have with that skill the better you become. Learning pascal will not get you a job at Google, but it's good practice and (in my opinion) fun!
